Dogs

Dogs are famous for their cute faces and soft fur. They're often called man's best friend.

You can find dogs throughout the world. They are popular pets and come in many different shapes and sizes. Over thousands of years, many different dog breeds have been created.

Some dogs are bred for hunting. These dogs run fast and have good endurance.

Dog breeds vary quite a bit. Some dogs, like chihuahuas, are very small, growing to be only about 5 pounds. Other dog breeds, like Great Danes, are very big.

Unfortunately, some species of dogs have been inbred, leading to health problems and birth defects.

Why dogs are great:

  • Dogs are great because they're really playful and cute!
  • Having a dog can relieve stress and encourage physical activity.
  • Dogs are smarter than hamsters and other small pets.
  • Dogs are used as emotional support animals, seeing eye dogs, and sniffer/police dogs, etc.
  • A dog can smell 1000 times better than a human!

Dogs also make great chauffers!

How dogs help us:

Besides working as emotional support animals and helping people who are blind or deaf, dogs are used in police work, drug searches, and SAR (search and rescue).

Dogs can be trained to sniff out drugs, working with policemen. They are helpful because of their amazing sense of smell.

Dogs can also be firefighter dogs and rescue people from burning buildings. They are also used for search and rescue because they can smell people who are buried under snow or rubble.
